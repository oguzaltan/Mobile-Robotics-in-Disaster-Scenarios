# Mobile Robotics in Disaster Scenarios
This repository contains my review paper and presentation about Mobile Robotics in Disaster Scenarios, submitted to Man-Machine Interaction Institute of RWTH Aachen, for the Seminar: Current Concepts and Trends in the Fields of Robotics and Simulation. 

## Content
1. Background and History of Mobile Robotics
2. Techniques and Systems Developed for Disaster Scenarios
   2.1 Unmanned Ground Vehicles (UGV)
   2.2 Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV)
   2.3 Unmanned Marine Vehicles (UMV)
3. Examples of Mobile Robots In Disaster Scenarios
   3.1 World Trade Center 9/11 Disaster
   3.2 Hurricanes Katrina, Rita and Wilma
   3.3 Fukushima Daiichi Nuclear Disaster
   3.4 Notre-Dame de Paris Fire
   3.5 ICARUS
4. The Future of Mobile Robotics in Disaster Scenarios
   4.1 Search and Rescue Robotics Competitions
   4.2 Trending Research Topics and Future Solutions
